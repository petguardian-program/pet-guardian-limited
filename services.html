Our Services

At Pet Guardian Limited, we offer a complete range of pet safety and care solutions designed to protect your furry companions and bring you peace of mind. From smart GPS tracking to personalized training advice, our mission is to make pet care simpler, smarter, and safer for every household.

🐾 Real-Time & Normal GPS Tracking

Stay connected to your pet at all times. Our PawSentry device provides real-time location updates every few seconds, and normal tracking every 1–2 minutes to conserve battery. See your pet’s position live on the app and even track multiple pets at once — each with unique colors and icons.

🗺️ Geofencing Zones

Define safe zones (like your home) and danger zones (such as roads or restricted areas) through our app. You’ll receive instant alerts whenever your pet leaves or enters these zones — ensuring you’re always one step ahead.

📶 WiFi Battery Saving

PawSentry intelligently switches to WiFi mode when your pet is home, reducing GPS usage and extending battery life. It automatically returns to GPS mode once your pet leaves the WiFi range — no manual effort needed.

📍 Location History

Curious where your pet has been today? View up to 90 days of detailed route history, including playtime spots and walking paths. Use date filters or a timeline slider to review your pet’s past adventures with ease.

🔔 Notifications & Alerts

Receive instant notifications for every important event — from geofence alerts to battery status. You’ll even get a weekly activity dashboard every Sunday, summarizing your pet’s movements and activity trends.

💡 Light & Sound Locator

Can’t find your pet in the dark or a crowded space? Activate your PawSentry’s LED light and sound feature right from the app. The function automatically stops after 2 minutes or can be manually turned off anytime.

🎤 Talkie Walkie Function

Communicate directly with your pet! Record short voice clips (up to 5 seconds) and send them to the tracker. You can also receive short audio recordings from your pet’s surroundings — a fun and reassuring way to stay close even from afar.

🔗 Share Live Tracking

Share your pet’s live location with friends or family. Each shared link allows others to track your pet safely in real time. Basic accounts allow two users, while premium accounts support up to five users.

❤️ Pet Safety Accessories

We also provide high-quality collars, reflective leashes, and ID tags — all designed for durability and safety. Each product is crafted with comfort and visibility in mind to keep your pet safe during day and night.

🚑 Emergency & Veterinary Support

Pet Guardian partners with trusted veterinary clinics and responders in Chattogram to provide quick support when emergencies strike. Our team can help guide you to the nearest vet or connect you with professional help instantly.

🎓 Training & Owner Guidance

We believe safety starts with awareness. That’s why we offer pet training tips, behavioral advice, and personalized guidance for owners — ensuring every pet grows in a safe and loving environment.
