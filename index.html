Our Flagship Product — PawSentry

Meet PawSentry — a next-generation GPS pet tracker designed and developed by Pet Guardian. PawSentry does more than track location — it keeps your pet safe, connected, and loved through intelligent features like:

  • Real-Time GPS Tracking — Track your pet’s live location anywhere, anytime.
  • Geofencing — Set safe zones and get instant alerts if your pet leaves or enters one.
  • WiFi Battery Saving — Smart power management when your pet is at home.
  • Location History — View your pet’s daily routes and favorite spots.
  • Light & Sound Locator — Find your pet easily in the dark or in crowded spaces.
  • Talkie Walkie — Communicate directly with your pet through recorded messages.
