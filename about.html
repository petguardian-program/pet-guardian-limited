Our Story

Pet Guardian began with a simple idea — to make pet safety effortless for families across Bangladesh. What started in Chattogram as a passion project has now grown into a trusted name in smart pet technology.

2019: Founded in Chattogram with a mission to protect pets using technology.
2021: Launched PawSentry — Bangladesh’s first smart GPS tracker for pets.
2023: Reached 1000+ pets across the country with reliable tracking and support.
2025: Expanded into pet care products and accessories, continuing our promise of love and protection.
