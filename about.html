About Pet Guardian Limited

Pet Guardian Limited is Bangladesh’s first tech-driven pet safety and care company — proudly based in Chattogram. We’re on a mission to make pet care smarter, safer, and full of love. From GPS tracking to intelligent alerts and safety zones, we design solutions that ensure every pet lives freely and every pet parent feels at peace.

Our Mission

To create a world where every tail feels safe. We believe that pets aren’t just animals — they’re family. That’s why we develop smart, reliable, and compassionate technology that keeps pets protected and connected wherever they go.

Our Vision

To become Bangladesh’s most trusted pet safety brand — blending innovation, empathy, and local expertise to build a better life for pets and the people who love them.

Our Story

Pet Guardian Limited was founded out of a simple but powerful belief — that technology can help save lives, even the furry ones. Based in Chattogram, we bring together a passionate team of engineers, designers, and animal lovers dedicated to crafting solutions that make a real difference. From smart pet trackers to emergency support and community-driven awareness, our goal is to make responsible pet ownership easier and more joyful.

Innovation in Action — PawSentry

Our flagship product, PawSentry, is a next-generation GPS pet tracker designed to give pet owners confidence and control. With real-time tracking, geofencing, WiFi battery saving, and two-way communication, PawSentry ensures your pet’s safety 24/7 — whether they’re at home or on an adventure.
